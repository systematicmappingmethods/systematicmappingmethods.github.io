6. Systematic mapping and stakeholder engagement: selling the concept and the outputs to end-users


This session will summarise the key benefits and outputs of systematic mapping, and how these can be explained to stakeholders, particularly funders and end-users. It will also introduce how systematic maps can be built on in further syntheses.


Learning objectives


  • To appreciate the key benefits of systematic maps for different stakeholders
  • To understand how to explain these benefits clearly
  • To appreciate how to communicate the findings from systematic maps to different stakeholders
